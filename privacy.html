Privacy Policy

Privacy, written for families.

Stories - Bedtime Adventures is built to be safe for children and respectful of families. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or track personal information.

Effective date: May 3, 2026
Contact: yuganshjain11@gmail.com

Summary

No accounts

Stories does not ask families to create an account or sign in.

No tracking

There are no analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking SDKs.

Local storage

Profile details, favourites, history, and settings stay on your device.

Information We Store

Stories may store the following information locally on your device so the app can personalize and remember your experience:

  • Child name or nickname entered during setup.
  • Age range and interests selected during setup.
  • Favourite stories or music tracks.
  • Playback history and app preferences.
  • Whether onboarding has already been completed.

This information is stored only on the device. We do not receive it, upload it, sell it, or share it.

Information We Do Not Collect

Stories does not collect or transmit:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials.
  • Precise location data.
  • Microphone recordings or camera data.
  • Advertising identifiers or analytics events.
  • Payment information.
  • Contacts, photos, calendars, or messages.

Children's Privacy

Stories is designed for use by families with children aged 2-7. The app is intended to be supervised by a parent or guardian. Because we do not collect personal information from children, we do not knowingly collect, sell, or disclose children's personal information.

Stories is designed with COPPA, GDPR-K, and similar child privacy principles in mind. If you believe information has been shared with us by mistake, contact us and we will help resolve the issue.

Third Parties

Stories does not include third-party advertising networks, third-party analytics SDKs, or social tracking tools. The app may use platform services provided by Apple, such as audio playback controls and local device storage, as part of normal app operation.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete local app data at any time by opening Settings in the app and choosing Reset app data. This removes the profile, favourites, history, and preferences stored by Stories.

Uninstalling the app also removes locally stored app data from the device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when the app changes or when legal requirements change. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
