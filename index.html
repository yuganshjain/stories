Dreamy Adventures

Classic bedtime stories and quiet soundscapes for peaceful family evenings, without ads or accounts.

Built for bedtime

Story time, music, and rest in one simple routine.

Bedtime works best when it feels predictable and unhurried. Stories is designed around that moment: choose a tale, listen together, follow along with text, or let calm music play while the room settles. The experience is intentionally quiet, friendly, and free from distracting sign-ins or advertising.

What is included

Everything needed for a calm night.

Classic stories

A library of fairy tales, animal stories, and gentle adventures chosen for family bedtime listening.

Calm music

Soft ambient tracks help bridge the space between story time and sleep.

Read-along text

Select stories include text so parents and children can read together at their own pace.

Sleep timer

The audio fades out gently after the chosen duration, avoiding a sudden stop.

Offline-first

Stories, music, fonts, and core features are bundled with the app so bedtime does not depend on a network connection.

No interruptions

No ads, no account creation, no analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

For families

Designed with young listeners in mind.

Stories is intended for personal family use with a parent or guardian nearby. The app uses a soft visual style, simple navigation, local favourites, local history, and a child profile that stays on the device.

  • Recommended for children aged 2-7.
  • Works well for bedtime, quiet time, and rest routines.
  • Profile, interests, favourites, and history are stored locally.
  • Reset app data removes locally stored profile and activity data.

Privacy promise

No ads. No accounts. No tracking.

Stories does not collect, sell, transmit, or track personal information. Any child profile details entered during setup are used only inside the app and remain on the device.
